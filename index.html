What Other People Can See

⚠️ WeekUp is built around social accountability. Please read this section carefully.

Depending on your settings, other authenticated users may see your username, avatar, bio, public habits and their titles, check-ins on public habits (including proof photos and captions), your streak counts on leaderboards, and claps and activity timestamps.

Proof Photo Access

Proof photos live in a private storage bucket. Access is enforced server-side: you can always see your own proofs; others may receive short-lived signed URLs only when policy allows — for example, today's proof in IST for a public habit from someone you follow.

What We Never Expose

Account Deletion

Delete your account via Me → Manage account → Delete account. This calls our delete_my_account function and cascades deletes across your profile, habits, check-ins, follows, and tokens.

⚠️ Storage files (proof photos, avatars) may take additional time to purge from backups or CDN caches. Razorpay may retain payment records under their own policy.

Security

HTTPS for all API and storage access
Row Level Security (RLS) so clients only access permitted rows
Private proof storage with signed, time-limited URLs

No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your phone and SMS messages secure. If you believe your account was compromised, contact privacy@weekup.app.

Your Choices & Rights

Correct inaccurate profile information (in-app edit)
Delete your account (in-app deletion)
Withdraw consent for optional processing (notifications, Contacts)
Object or restrict certain processing where law provides
Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
